Why Visit Puerto Princesa?

Puerto Princesa is home to the world-famous Underground River, stunning beaches, and rich biodiversity. Explore our natural wonders and experience unforgettable adventures!

  • Visit the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park
  • Relax at Honda Bay's pristine beaches
  • Experience local culture and cuisine
